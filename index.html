Our Mission

Our mission is to offer hope and healing to veterans suffering from traumatic brain injuries (TBI) and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) through hyperbaric oxygen therapy.

Hyperbaric Oxygen Treatment

Hyperbaric oxygen therapy involves breathing pure oxygen in a pressurized chamber. This treatment has been shown to promote healing, reduce inflammation, and improve cognitive function.
